Admissions

Welcome to the Admissions page! Here, you can apply for admissions, check your application status, and find all necessary information regarding our admission process.

How to Apply

To apply for admission, please follow these steps:

Check Eligibility: Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria for the desired course.

Complete the Application Form: Fill out the online application form available on our website.

Submit Required Documents: Upload necessary documents such as transcripts, ID proof, and other relevant certificates.

Pay the Application Fee: Make the payment through our secure portal.

Submit Your Application: Review all details and submit your application.

After submitting, you can check your admission status on this page.
