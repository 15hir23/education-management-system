Scholarship Opportunities

Explore our various scholarship programs designed to support students who demonstrate academic excellence, leadership skills, and community involvement. Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria before applying.

Available Scholarship Programs

We offer several scholarships, including:

Merit-Based Scholarships

Awarded to students with outstanding academic performance.

Need-Based Scholarships

Designed for students who demonstrate financial need.

Leadership Scholarships

For students who have shown exceptional leadership in their schools or communities.

Sports Scholarships

For athletes who excel in their respective sports.

Make sure to check the eligibility criteria for each program before applying.

How to Apply

Follow these steps to apply for a scholarship:

  1. Check Eligibility: Ensure you meet the requirements for the desired scholarship.
  2. Complete the Application Form: Fill out the online application form available on our website.
  3. Submit Required Documents: Upload your transcripts, ID proof, and any other relevant certificates.
  4. Submit Your Application: Review all details and submit your application.

Important Dates

Mark your calendars for the following important dates:

  • Application Start Date: January 1, 2024
  • Application Deadline: March 31, 2024
  • Notification of Awards: April 15, 2024
  • Start of Academic Year: August 1, 2024
