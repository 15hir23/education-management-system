About Christ University

Christ University, known for its commitment to providing holistic education, offers various courses across different streams. It has four main campuses located in Bangalore, each offering unique facilities and a wide range of academic programs to cater to the diverse needs of students.

Additional Information

Christ University, founded in 1969, has been recognized for its academic excellence and quality education. The university emphasizes holistic development, providing students with numerous opportunities for personal and professional growth.

With a vibrant campus life, students engage in various extracurricular activities, including sports, cultural events, and community service initiatives. The university's commitment to research and innovation further enhances its reputation as a leading educational institution.
